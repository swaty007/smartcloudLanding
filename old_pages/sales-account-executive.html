Sales Account Executive

ROLE DESCRIPTION: Invisible.io is seeking a dynamic and motivated Sales Account Executive for our sales team. The Sales Account Executive will be responsible for assisting small- to medium-sized businesses to sell their products to our customers. This person will be the primary point of contact for those companies throughout the entire sales process.
The Sales Account Executive will be expected to develop and manage a transactional sales pipeline with a high volume of accounts while executing sales strategies to secure deals that will meet aggressive selection and revenue goals. This individual will be responsible for identifying and building relationships with key influencers and decision makers within the prospective accounts, along with internal stakeholders and cross-functional teams to create and present compelling Invisible.io solutions that meet and exceed customer requirements..

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • 3+ years inside sales experience
  • Attention to detail. You prioritize multiple tasks simultaneously without sacrificing the ability to dive deep.
  • Relentless customer focus—innovating and insisting on high standards on our customers’ behalf
  • Experience using analytical, sales, and productivity tools including Salesforce, Tableau, and Excel

Preferred Qualifications:

  • B2B Sales Experience
  • Bachelor's Degree
  • Ability to thrive in an ambiguous environment
  • Ability to prioritize and manage multiple responsibilities
