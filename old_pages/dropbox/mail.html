Enhanced enterprise-class scheduling

Now, booking meetings through emails is even more effortless. You can match everyone's calendars in seconds by selecting your periods of availability, leveraging a personal meeting booking page, and more.

Easily escalate incoming emails to cases and to the right queues. Set your custom context rules to automatically prefill case-related information, create reminders in accordance to SLA response times, and much more - all right from your Inbox.

We've teamed up with a leading document management company, SpringCM, to let you clinch enterprise-class deals in an even more neat way. Now, whenever you need to send an email, all of your documents can be within a 1-click reach.
