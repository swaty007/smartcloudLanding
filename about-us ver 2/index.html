Our Vision

We wanted to bridge the gap between how enterprise systems work and how people work every day. We aspired making people more productive and successful by saving their valuable time and streamlining their workflows. To leverage all resources and life-work balance of employees, it was necessary to undertake a smooth melding of corporate and private environments. This insight marked the official beginning of the company in 2005.

Our Mission

Data integration being our first core mission; process integration follows in tracks. You have already got expectations and predefined workflows you favor, and really great customer-oriented products preserve them intact to facilitate native look and smooth feel. Integration should be invisible. Thus, here abide our vision and mission.
