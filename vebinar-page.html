What you'll learn

At SmartCloud Connect, our focus is on increasing productivity and nearly all features we implement in the product are done with that in mind. Join us for an inside look at how we help our customers in making their teams more productive when it comes to Inbox / Calendar and Salesforce synergy. We'll be happy to share how you can improve your routine CRM and Inbox-driven tasks by integrating your actual business processes, besides the data.

We'll cover how to best:

  • Keep your CRM data fresh, with a rule-based, semi-automatic, and case-driven sync
  • Reveal the CRM data of your prospects & customers with Salesforce in your Office 365, Outlook / Gmail;
  • Schedule meetings effortlessly, matching everyone's calendars in seconds
  • Customize every angle of our product to meet the workflows of your business;
  • Discover some helpful extras to spend more time using your data, not creating it.
