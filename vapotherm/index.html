Besides, Vapotherm needed great quality access to CRM data for field personnel, seamless integration with Office 365 and total mobility.

Implementation

To meet the challenges most efficiently, Invisible.io implemented SmartCloud Connect, the best integration solution to streamline Vapotherm workflow.
